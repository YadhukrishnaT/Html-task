A.P.J Abdul kalam

Great Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the Development of india's missile and nuclear weapons programs.

About the Legend

  • A.P.J Abdul.kalam in full Avul Pakir Jainuladeen Abdul Kalam, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu, India.

  • He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

  • Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology and in 1958 joined the Defense Research and Development Organisation(DRDO)

  • Rejoining DRDO in 1982 ,Kalam planned the program that produced a number of successfull missiles, which helped earn him the nickname Missile Man

  • Wheeler Island, a national Institute of Management Shillong, Kalam collapsed and died from cardiac arrest on 27 July 2015, aged 83

  • A prominent road in New Delhi was renamed from Aurangzeb Road to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Road in August 2015
